BrainTraining is a simple application that allows you to keep your brain in good shape and expand its capabilities.

How?

By solving simple examples

The basis for the creation of the application was the study described in the book “Train Your Brain” by Ryuta Kawashima. (Read it, if you are interested ðŸ˜‰).

The main idea is that solving simple examples is extremely useful!

In the book the author proves this with the help of modern methods of functional neuroimaging,from which it follows that communication with people, creative manual labor, and, of course, reading, writing and counting naturally activate the work of the prefrontal cortex of the brain, that controls higher nervous activity. This is confirmed by images obtained using optical topography.
